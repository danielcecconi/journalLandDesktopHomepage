Who we are:

Journal Land is a home run passion project from a small team in Perth, Australia. At Journal Land our goal is to provide the highest quality bullet journal supplies from around the world, with prompt delivery and fantastic service. Our ambition is to create a place where people can come and be inspired to create in the best way possible. From our Perth home to you are all the products that we enjoy and love, and that make bullet journaling so much fun.
